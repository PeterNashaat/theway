The Way

The Way

A non-profit, non-governmental organization stands for the even-handed care about unprivileged people by providing them high quality educational, social, developmental services and medical care to help them live with dignity, accomplish their mission, fulfill their dreams, and sustain a fruitful healthy life.

Vision

Creating a world of hope where the less privileged have access to equitable life-improving opportunities to live with dignity and security

Mission

To provide relentless care to everyone every day with best quality services with the spirit of love, respect, dignity, and compassion to all

Hospital

The Way Hospital project aims to offer comprehensive, high-quality healthcare in Cairo's underserved Al-Marg district. Equipped with essential utilities, electricity, water, and hospital gas supplies for clinics, ICUs, and operation rooms, it focuses on disease prevention, management, and health education for women, children, adults, and seniors.

Shelter!

A compassionate shelter in an underprivileged Egyptian community provides a safe haven for elderly residents, while empowering disabled individuals through transformative courses, fostering positive change and social inclusion.

About us

The Way Foundation for Care and Sustainable Development (WCD) was founded 2021 to care about unprivileged people whether medically, educationally or socially to help them live with dignity, accomplish their mission, fulfill their dreams and have a better fruitful healthy life. All of this is meant to be achieved through our many projects including well-equipped hospital, generously-served residential home for the elderly and handicapped, as well as a professionally-prepared developmental program (be perfect program) for the teens for a better promising future.
